The capital is the Empire's argument in stone. Caeldrion is built in concentric rings — walls, garden, market, civic, residential — each ring a layer of ordered depth around the Citadel at the center, where [the Sovereign](/icons/the-sovereign) keeps a working court. Visitors expecting imperial excess find instead the same doctrine that governs the [Grand Army](/grand-army): meritocracy, uniformity, economy, expressed at the scale of a city.

## The Citadel

The innermost ring holds the Citadel: throne room, the Grand Council chamber, and the Inner Sanctum where the [Purple Guard's](/grand-army/purple-guard) Aegis Watch is headquartered. The throne itself is war-steel — burnished silver, not gold — and the Archive confirms the popular story: it predates the current dynasty, and no Sovereign has permitted it to be replaced.

## Institutions of the Rings

- **The Imperial War Academy** — the Empire's officer forge, whose blind examinations feed every division. [The Sovereign](/icons/the-sovereign) graduated its command track at twenty-two.
- **The Imperial College** — where casters are rated against the [threshold scale](/arcanum/mana-wells) and the [Weave](/arcanum/the-weave) is taught as a certified trade.
- **The Imperial Grand Library** — this Archive's own house, holding the Codex of which these records are the public face.
- **The Craftsman District** — workshops of the [Craftsman Corps](/grand-army/craftsman), where war-steel is channel-cut and the treatment baths run day and night.

## The Promenade

The Grand Promenade runs the city's full radius, gate to Citadel — the road on which the army parades, petitioners walk, and the [Ascension procession](/history/ascension-of-the-sovereign) passed in silence fourteen years ago. By statute its width is one full company front. The Empire plans even its ceremonies to march.
