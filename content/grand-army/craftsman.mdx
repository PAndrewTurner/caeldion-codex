Ask any veteran which cloth they were gladdest to see and the answer is rarely a fighting color. The Craftsman Corps — Medical, Science, and Engineering under one charter — is the division that turns the Empire's doctrine of [Economy](/grand-army) into practice: nothing wasted, least of all soldiers.

## Mandate

The Corps holds three directorates, each wearing the steel-teal cloth:

- **Medical Directorate** — forward surgeries, Burn-trauma wards, and the field protocols that made [the Burn](/arcanum/the-burn) a treated injury rather than a sentence.
- **Engineering Directorate** — roads, bridges, siege works, and the fortifications program from [Fort Dawnshield](/geography/the-sunlit-isles) to the [Highfjord](/geography/highfjord) cliff batteries.
- **Science Directorate** — the runic development laboratories: [heat-sink armor channels](/arcanum/runic-armor), [arming doublet](/arcanum/arming-doublets) treatment baths, and the [mana well](/arcanum/mana-wells) survey standard.

## Selection

The Corps examines for craft before it drills for war. Candidates present a masterwork — a set surgery, a bridge calculation, a rune matrix — before a blind board, in the same meritocratic form as every division. Its members are soldiers entire: Corps field companies march armored, and its plate is engraved with the maker's guild-mark, the one ornament the [Sea of Silver](/grand-army) doctrine permits, because it is a certification, not a decoration.

## Notable Works

The Corps' honor roll is built infrastructure: the Five Bridges themselves — rebuilt to Corps standard after [the battle that bears their name](/history/battle-of-the-five-bridges) — the Capital's [ring fortifications](/geography/caeldrion), and the third-generation heat-sink channel now standard on every suit of war-steel in the army.

> Archivist's note: Science Directorate manuals are the most-requested documents in this Archive. The Directorate's standing reply is appended to each: "The manual is free. The masterwork examination is not waived."
