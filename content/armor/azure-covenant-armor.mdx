The [Azure Covenant's](/grand-army/azure-covenant) armor is the Leather Kit doctrine taken to its most demanding application. Steel Blue cloth over rune-imbued silver leather, it carries the same full rune system as war-steel plate — identical heat management, passive ignition, and magical amplification described in [runic armor](/arcanum/runic-armor). The choice of leather over war-steel is purely about somatic casting freedom, not protection or magical capability: a Covenant caster's arms describe the largest gestures in the Army's magical repertoire, and no articulated pauldron survives contact with that requirement.

Where the [Ranger Leather Kit](/armor/ranger-leather-kit) and the [Ghost Kit](/armor/ghost-kit) are dyed to disappear, the Covenant's leather is finished silver and worn openly — the Covenant does not hide. It glows along its inscription circuits when the [Coven Link](/grand-army/azure-covenant) runs at full output, and it hardens to steel density on impact like every leather configuration in the Army. Heavy Runic Gauntlets — the most advanced casting tools in the Army outside of Purple — are the primary weapon; the [runic sword](/armor/sidearms-and-weapons) is carried but drawn only in desperation.

## The Two Configurations

- **War Plate — field and court.** For formal occasions and the rare engagements where a Covenant member stands inside the enemy's reach, the Covenant wears the standard [Steel War Plate](/armor/steel-war-plate) in steel-blue cloth with gold filigree, like every specialist division.
- **The Arcanist field configuration.** The working armor of the Covenant: rune-imbued silver leather cut for unrestricted shoulder, arm, and hand movement. This is what a Coven wears when it is doing what a Coven exists to do — sustained bombardment at outputs no single caster could survive unassisted.

## Engineering for Output, Not Impact

Most armor in the Army is engineered against what the enemy does to the wearer. Covenant armor is engineered first against what the wearer does to themselves. Coven-linked casting channels the combined output of an entire team through individual bodies, and the leather's inscription circuits are the heat-sink that keeps [the Burn](/arcanum/the-burn) survivable at outputs that would kill an unarmored caster outright. The Covenant Gauntlets extend the same principle to the hands: engineered to focus raw mana into city-leveling spells while protecting the caster from their own output.

## Protection Doctrine

The armor's design assumes the [Iron Legion](/grand-army/iron-legion) is doing its job. Covenant doctrine places Covens behind a protected line — the [Black Legion protection doctrine](/grand-army/azure-covenant) — precisely because the Arcanist configuration trades melee survivability for casting output. A Coven whose line holds is the most destructive formation in the Army; a Coven caught in the open is a group of specialists in leather. The armor is honest about which of those situations it was built for.
