The Cobalt Ranger Kit is a [Leather Kit](/armor/war-steel-doctrine#the-three-armor-kits) dyed dark navy blue, worn by the [Cobalt Rangers](/grand-army/cobalt-ascendancy#the-cobalt-rangers) on stealth deployments. The dark navy carries their cobalt identity into a color that disappears in low light — a Maestro does not stop being [Cobalt Ascendancy](/grand-army/cobalt-ascendancy) when they take the field in this kit, they simply stop being visible while doing it.

The [Cobalt Ascendancy](/grand-army/cobalt-ascendancy) presents the widest armor variety of any division — [War Plate](/armor/steel-war-plate) in multiple configurations, [Light Kit](/armor/light-steel-kit) with cloak, and this exclusive Ranger Kit, worn only by Maestros who have completed the tri-track Ranger qualification. It is not issued to Cobalt Officers, and it is not available on request — it is earned through the same qualification pathway that produces the Army's most complete Developed Path candidates for the [Violet Trials](/grand-army/purple-guard#selection-the-violet-trials).

## The Cloak

Like the standard Light Kit, the Cobalt Ranger Kit is issued with a hooded cloak — cut for silent movement and worn raised or lowered depending on the mission's need for peripheral vision against total concealment.

## Doctrine

The Ranger Kit is what makes a [Cobalt Ranger](/grand-army/cobalt-ascendancy#the-cobalt-rangers) tactically distinct from either a standard Maestro or a standard Ranger: full Weave capability carried in a silhouette built for reconnaissance and deep-territory operations, rather than the pitched-battle profile of the Ascendancy's usual War Plate presence. It is the clearest physical expression of the Army's cross-division qualification pathways — proof, worn on the body, that a soldier has mastered two disciplines rather than one.

> Archivist's note: for the qualification pathway that earns this kit, see [the Cobalt Rangers](/grand-army/cobalt-ascendancy#the-cobalt-rangers) section of the Cobalt Ascendancy entry. For the base leather doctrine this kit is dyed from, see [the Leather Kit](/armor/war-steel-doctrine#the-three-armor-kits).
